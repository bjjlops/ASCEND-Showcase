# Roadmap

A high-level, honest view of where ASCEND is and where it's heading. Dates are intentionally
omitted — this is a solo-led project, and the order matters more than the calendar.

## Where it is today

- 🟢 **Web Planner — live.** Free in early access at [ascenddaily.app](https://ascenddaily.app).
- 🟢 **Marketing site — live.** Honest product presentation; payments scaffolded but disabled.
- 🔵 **Trader — research preview.** Research, risk validation, simulation, and journaling. No
  execution.
- 🟡 **APEX — in development.** Planner-first intelligence behind the private AI boundary.
- 🟡 **Mobile Planner — in development.** Native iOS & Android; no store links until published.
- ✅ **Platform foundations — done.** Monorepo, shared contracts, security boundaries, audit, and
  the safety posture are in place.

## In progress

- Deepening **APEX** planning and review quality (still deterministic-first, still no invention).
- Hardening the **mobile** experience toward a publishable beta.
- Expanding **Trader** research and risk tooling within the no-execution boundary.

## Considered, deliberately gated

- **Billing** — the checkout path is scaffolded but switched off. It turns on only when there's
  something worth charging for and the flow is genuinely complete.
- **Any connected trading execution** — gated behind the written safety checklist in
  **[security-and-safety.md](security-and-safety.md)**. Until every item is met, execution stays
  manual and in the user's own broker.

## Principles that won't change

Whatever ships next, these hold:

1. Apps never hold privileged keys.
2. AI never invents your data.
3. Trader never trades for you without explicit, gated, reviewed safety.
4. Marketing only advertises what's real.

> The roadmap is a direction, not a promise. ASCEND would rather ship one honest thing than
> announce ten.
